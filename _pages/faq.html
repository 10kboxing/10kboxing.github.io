I am already signed to another agency.

That is great news, that shows us that you are a committed and experienced boxer.  we work with everyone and  are happy to work with your  team to  get you more paid fights. Just think of us as a bonus.

 

I have no boxing experience, can I still join?

10K BOXING always matches boxers of the same calibre. If you do not have any experience and would like to fight, then no worries, our database  also includes boxers who have never boxed before. If you would like to gain more experience, 10K BOXING can inform you of upcoming sparring events which will not only help you get more experience but will also enable you to network with other promoters.

Why not sign up to our newsletter which updates you  of upcoming shows, sparring trials and other opportunities.

 

Does 10K deal with MMA and Kickboxing?

10k boxing does not only deal with boxing. We deal with a range of styles such as MMA, Kickboxing, Muay Thai and many others.

 

How, when and how much do I get paid?

All fighters will be paid directly after their fight by a 10K BOXING agent. The amount of their purse depends on the show and the promoter.

Payment for ticket sales are usually paid at the end of the night however can be sooner depending on the promoter.

 

How do I join 10K BOXING Agency?

Signing up to 10K BOXING is easy. All you have to do is (Click Here) and enter your details. Once we receive the information, an agent will contact you to discuss your experience as well as possible opportunities we may have for you.

 

Why do I contribute 15% to 10K BOXING ?

10k boxing has noticed a gap in the market where boxers are either not getting paid enough, or not being matched with boxers with the same calibre. We do all the hard work so you don't have to,  like securing you paid fights,  negotiating contracts and doing all the boring stuff

 

I am a promoter, how do I book talent?

To book talent, all you have to do is simply click on the talent’s  profile and complete the booking form on their page.  If you are booking more than one talent then simply give us a email or a call to get your requirments for your upcoming events. Please note that promoters are charged a £30 boxing fee per boxer, excluding the fighter's purse. If you are looking to book a model/ ring girl, simply select and book your choice of model and we will be in touch.

 

I am a model/ring girl, can I join?

10K BOXING  understands the importance of visual imagery for each white collar & semi pro boxing events and having the right eye-catching, glamorous look for the crowd to enjoy. If you would like to model or be a 10K ring girl, simply send us an email and we will be in touch.

 

What happens if I can not fight or have to cancel?

All boxers are required to inform a 10K BOXING  agent  are unable to fight as soon as possible. In the event where a boxer falls sick, 10K BOXING may request a doctor's note as proof. In circumstances where boxers fail to turn up, 10K BOXING  may seek legal action to cover the damages lost.

 

Will I be covered by medical insurance?

As 10K BOXING is merely an agency, fighters are required to discuss medical insurance with promoters. Please note that fighters are required to attend a compulsory medical check prior to their fight. In circumstances where a fighter is declared unfit to take part, they will be required to forfeit.
