10K Media is a newly formed entertainment company specializing in the promotion of Amateur Boxing events in the UK. The company was founded in 2016 by Bady Touray, himself an amateur boxer with over 12 years discipline.

With more than ten years experience in the music industry as a musician (rapper) and model, Bady’s entrepreneural vision taps into his varied and interesting background infusing style and passion in this new and exciting venture. We have more than 400 registered fighters and models on our books with new additions weekly.

Our aim is to elevate10K Media to a relevant and significant platform for matchmaking Boxers & MMA fighters and to provide the best possible match-ups for combat sports events across the UK. From matchmaking fighters and assisting them with their specific needs, to booking models for a music video or modelling photoshoot for magazines, 10K Media has the right talent to suit your occasion.
