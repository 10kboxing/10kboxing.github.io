10K Boxing is a match-making agency specializing in match-making white-collar fighters for promoters across the UK. The agency was founded in 2016 by Bady Touray, himself an amateur boxer with over 12 years discipline. We have more than 250 registered fighters on our books with new additions weekly.

Our aim is to elevate 10K Boxing to a relevant and significant platform for matchmaking Boxers & MMA fighters and to provide the best possible match-ups for combat sporting events across the UK. 10K Boxing has the right fighter to suit your occasion.
